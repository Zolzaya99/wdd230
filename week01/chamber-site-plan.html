Site Name

Downtown Chicago Tour Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this sight is to inform people about what Downtown Chicago has to offer through a camber of commerce style website. Photos and design will be used to persuade individuals who come across the sight to stop and learn more aout the area. Local information will be given and people will easily be able to see all that Downtown Chicago has to offer.

Scenerios

The target audience of this website is individuals who are anywhere from 20 - 30 years of age. It will be targeted to young and adventurous people who aren't afraid to explore and try new things. The site will feature very modern design and style in order to reach out to the younger audience.
